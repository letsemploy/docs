# Let us know and we track your jobs

We provide a free web service called **SourceTracker** to fetch and verify ojobpub.json data regularly

Visit [sources.letsemploy.org](https://sources.letsemploy.org) to add your domain.

## Purpose

With this data, it allow us to provide:

- Statistics about adoption and job counts.
- Build a downloadable archive of all fetched healthy ojobpub.json once a day for everyone to download and use. 

## Fetching Interval

- SourceTracker visits and downloads your the ojobpub.json from your website once every 24h.
- If it fails, it will retry every few hours.
- If if fails for 7, 30 and 60 days, an email to webmaster@<your-domain> will be send.
- If it fails for 90 days, your domain will be removed from the tracker.
