# Let us track your jobs

We provide a free web service called **Source Tracker** to fetch ojobpub.json data regularly. 

Visit https://tracker.letsemploy.org.

## Purpose

With this data, it allow us to provide:

- Statistics about adoption and job counts
- Build a package (zip) of all fetched ojobpub.json once a day for download.

## Fetching Interval

- Tracker visits and downloads your the ojobpub.json from your website once every 24h.
- If if fails to fetch for 7, 30 and 60 days, an email to webmaster@<your-domain> will be send.
- If it fails to fetch for 90 days, your domain will be removed from the tracker.
